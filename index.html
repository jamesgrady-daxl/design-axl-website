Design Axl is led by James Grady, a Boston-based designer and educator with nearly two decades of broad-based experience.

Design Axl goes beyond the banal. We are a boutique practice that helps organizations build their brands and visually communicate the data at the heart of their businesses. We’ve worked with cutting-edge clients in healthcare, technology, food/beverage, and other industries, providing them with print and interactive design, UI/UX, information design, and data visualization.

James’ design approach is a powerful combination of theory and execution. Informed by an empirically based methodology that he developed as part of his MFA thesis at Rhode Island School of Design, it encourages a deeper level of creative exploration, which he balances with an unparalleled understanding of brand and visual communication.
